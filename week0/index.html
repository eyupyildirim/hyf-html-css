About AmbitiousRefugees

AmbitiousRefugees is non-profit organization that functions as a bridge between highly educated refugees and companies, organizations and governmental agencies. We help refugees to find courses, jobs and activities in the direction of their interests.

Top 5 Open Course for Refugees in Netherlands 2019

Are you a refugee or an asylum seeker in Netherlands? Eager to improve yourself and adopt the Dutch community by attending courses. Let's have a look at top 5 open course for refugees in Netherlands 2019.

1. HackYourFuture


A meaningful photo is took at HackYourFuture modern class.


HackYourFuture trains refugees to become full-stack software developers. Our coding school teaches a 6-month web development program to refugees with or without a background in coding. The curriculum is created and taught by our volunteer network of over forty professional developers. They teach our students how to work on projects in a modern tech team. How to be problem-solvers. At HackYourFuture, students learn to think like developers. Training refugees in computer programming significantly boosts their chances of fulfilling, long-term employment. Plus, it fills a gap on the job market. Everyone wins. Our approach works: 96% of our graduates have gone on to find jobs in the tech industry.
For more information

Duration6 Months
PriceFree of Charge
LocationAmsterdam

2. Future Learn Dutch Language Course



Over three weeks you will learn Dutch from experts at the University of Groningen in the Netherlands. You’ll learn how to introduce yourself and talk and write about family, friends, work, study and daily live. Your basic Dutch language skills should make living or visiting the Netherlands even more enjoyable. This course is aimed at people with no prior knowledge of the Dutch language, who have a strong interest in learning languages. It will give you a taste of language skills that will help you to survive in Dutch-speaking countries. The main focus will be on receptive skills like reading and listening. It will be taught in English.
For more information

Duration5 Weeks
PriceFree of Charge
LocationOnline


3. Engineers for Refugees

Engineers for Refugees is set up to offer an accelerated integration course to young and ambitious refugees. E4R is an initiative set up by students of TU Delft to help fellow students that had to leave their country. Every November a new class of refugees start with our program. The goal is to teach as much about the Dutch language and culture as possible within a year. In collaboration with the TU Delft and VluchtelingenWerk Nederland (Dutch refugee organisation) E4R is currently hosting a class of 20 students!
For more information

Duration1 Year
PriceContact with Organization
LocationDelft

4. Inclusion by Utrecht University

Utrecht University’s Incluusion is aimed at those students with refugee backgrounds who are currently 'on hold' in emergency shelters, refugee centers or in their new houses. It offers newcomers the opportunity to participate for free in all kinds of courses offered by Utrecht University. Incluusion is a call to action to make use of this waiting period. Refugees with academic backgrounds and proper levels of English are more than welcome to apply.
For more information










Duration6 Weeks
PriceFree of Charge
LocationUtrecht


5. Creative and Academic English Course

Creative writing is an art which for some comes naturally, and for others, learning refines this talent. UID created this course to enable some newcomers who wish to write about their stories or those who simply want to use their passion for creative writing to make their contribution to the society; while keeping in mind the fact that writing can be therapeutic, not only for refugees, asylum seekers or victims of different crimes, but for just about anyone. For the sake of efficiency, we have ensured a small class size, and a one-on-one approach, giving each participant the opportunity to bring out the best in them as far as creative writing goes. Participants have the opportunity to learn how to write books of different genres, poems, and other types of creative writing of their choice.
For more information

Duration1 Week
PriceFree of Charge
LocationDen Haag
